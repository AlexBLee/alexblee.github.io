Chess
Project Details / Key Highlights
A recreation of the classic game of Chess.
  • Built with Unity in C#
  • Integrates a well-known Chess Bot/Engine called Stockfish to play against in singleplayer
  • Utilizes the Photon Network Library to play online against other players.
